Sabalan

Sabalan (Persian: سبلان [sæbæ'lɒːn]) is an inactive stratovolcano in Ardabil Province of northwestern Iran. At 4,794 metres (15,728 ft) in elevation, it is the third-highest mountain in Iran. It has a permanent crater lake formed at its summit. On one of its slopes around 3,600 metres (11,800 ft) in elevation there are large rock formations of eroded volcanic outcrops that resemble animals, birds, and insects.
